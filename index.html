HOW WE ACTUALLY WORK

A few things that work for us

  • Write a job description, not a prompt. Role · owns · inputs · steps · never · escalate. The contract beats cleverness.
  • Automate one repeatable task at a time. Pick the boring thing that eats an hour a day. Ship it, then add the next.
  • Make agents fire on triggers, not chats. Email lands → work happens. No human in the loop until it escalates.
  • Validate before you build. One falsifiable bar, written before a dollar is spent. Try to kill it first.
  • Hire humans only where they win. Headcount is the last resort, not the default. Scale through agents.

None of this needs a platform. It's Claude, a runtime, and your judgment written down as rubrics. Pick one of these and try it.
